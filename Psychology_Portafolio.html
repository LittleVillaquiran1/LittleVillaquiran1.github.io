I'm a Psychologist and Graphic Designer

Laura Varela is a Graphic Design student who is training skills in the field of illustration and artistic design, close to graduating as a Graphic Designer and Web Designer in November 2021. Since previous years, Laura has dedicated herself to the work of psychology, which allows her to have a more sensitive and objective view of design, understanding human behavior and its diverse needs at a cultural and social level. Laura has always been passionate about her work on a social level and her creativity, which has allowed her to develop artistic and flexible tools in the educational field favoring diversity and social inclusion. Within her medium-term projects, she hopes to be part of a creative company where she can integrate the two elements of his professional training, psychology and graphic design - illustration.

Psychologist specialized in the social and educational area with experience in the application of ABA behavioral therapy. My passion lies with my patients and their families, for whom I care a lot..
