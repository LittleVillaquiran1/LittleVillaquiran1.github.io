About me

Hi, my name is Laura! and I am a freelance designer student. Welcome to my amazing page. My work mostly focuses on hand drawn illustrations from real life photo's. Since previous years, I have dedicated myself to the work of psychology, which allows her to have a more sensitive and objective view of design, understanding human behavior and its diverse needs at a cultural and social level. I have always been passionate about my work on a social level and my creativity, which has allowed me to develop artistic and flexible tools in the educational field favoring diversity and social inclusion. Within my medium-term projects, I hope to be part of a creative company where I can integrate my two elements of this professional training, psychology and graphic design - illustration.To see more of my work click bellow.
